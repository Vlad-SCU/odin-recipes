Lasagna

Description

In this classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned.

Ingredients

  • Beef and Sausage
  • Mozazarlla
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Parsely
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  • Boil Pasta
  • Prepare Meat Sauce
  • Combine Cheese Mixture
  • Layer and bake
